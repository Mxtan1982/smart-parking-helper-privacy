Smart Parking Helper 隐私权政策

最后更新日期：2025-11-29

1. 应用简介

「Smart Parking Helper」（以下简称「本应用」）由 MM Lucky / Kiing Jiu Hock 开发，是一款用于记录停车地点与楼层的工具应用。本应用为一次性付费购买应用，不含广告、不含第三方追踪 SDK。

2. 我们收集哪些数据？

本应用不会收集、储存或上传任何可识别个人身份的信息（例如姓名、电邮、电话号码等）。

本应用只在本机使用以下数据：

2.1 位置信息（Location）

  • 用途：取得你当前的 GPS 位置，以记录停车地点，并在稍后协助你导航回车子位置。
  • 使用方式：仅用于在本机显示及保存你的停车点，不会上传到任何服务器，也不会与第三方分享。

2.2 手动输入的文字（楼层、备注等）

  • 这些文字仅储存在你的装置本机，用于显示给你自己查看。
  • 开发者无法远端存取这些内容。

3. 数据储存与传输

  • 本应用不使用云端服务器，也不会自动上传你的数据到互联网。
  • 所有数据（停车位置、楼层备注）仅储存在你的装置本地。
  • 当你卸载应用或清除应用资料时，这些本地数据也会一并被删除。

4. 第三方服务

  • 本应用不集成广告 SDK（例如 AdMob）。
  • 本应用不集成分析 SDK（例如 Firebase Analytics、Google Analytics 等）。
  • 本应用不会分享或出售任何用户数据给第三方。

5. 儿童隐私

  • 本应用面向一般驾驶人士，并不是专门为 13 岁以下儿童设计。
  • 我们不会刻意收集 13 岁以下儿童的任何个人资料。
  • 如监护人认为儿童不适合使用本应用，可自行在装置上移除本应用。

6. 权限说明

本应用可能会向系统申请以下权限：

6.1 位置权限（Approximate / Precise Location）

  • 用途：记录停车位置及协助导航回车。
  • 不会用于背景追踪行踪，不会用于广告投放。

你可随时在装置系统设置中关闭本应用的定位权限。若关闭定位，本应用仍可使用文字备注楼层，但无法自动保存 GPS 停车点。

7. 数据安全

  • 由于本应用不上传资料到服务器，远端资料外泄风险极低。
  • 建议你为装置设置锁屏密码、指纹或人脸辨识，以保护装置内的本地数据。

8. 本政策的更新

随着功能调整或法律要求变更，我们可能会更新本隐私权政策。更新后的版本会发布于此页面，并以「最后更新日期」标示。

Privacy Policy for Smart Parking Helper

Last updated: 2025-11-29

1. About the App

Smart Parking Helper (the “App”) is developed by MM Lucky / Kiing Jiu Hock. It helps users save their parking location and floor information. The App is a paid, one-time purchase and does not contain ads or third-party tracking SDKs.

2. Data we collect

The App does not collect, store, or upload any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, or phone number.

The App only uses the following data on your device:

2.1 Location data

  • Purpose: to get your current GPS location in order to save your parking spot and help you navigate back to your car.
  • Usage: location data is used only to display and store your parking spot locally on your device. It is not uploaded to any server and not shared with any third party.

2.2 User-entered notes

  • Text notes such as floor or comments are stored locally on your device and are shown only to you.
  • The developer cannot access this data remotely.

3. Data storage and transfer

  • The App does not use any cloud backend and does not automatically upload your data to the internet.
  • All data (parking locations, notes) is stored locally on your device.
  • When you uninstall the App or clear its data, the locally stored data will be removed.

4. Third-party services

  • The App does not include advertising SDKs (e.g. AdMob).
  • The App does not include analytics SDKs (e.g. Firebase Analytics, Google Analytics).
  • The App does not share or sell any user data to third parties.

5. Children’s privacy

  • The App is intended for general users (drivers) and is not specifically directed to children under 13.
  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
  • Parents or guardians may remove the App from the device if they believe it is not appropriate for a child.

6. Permissions

The App may request the following permission:

6.1 Location (approximate / precise)

  • Used only to save your parking location and help you navigate back to your car.
  • Not used for background tracking or advertising.

You can revoke location permission at any time in your device settings. If you disable location, you can still use manual notes, but the App cannot automatically save GPS coordinates.

7. Data security

  • Because the App does not upload data to any server, the risk of remote data breach is very low.
  • We recommend that you protect your device with a screen lock (PIN, fingerprint, or face ID) to secure local data.

8. Changes to this Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time due to new features or legal requirements. Updates will be published on this page with an updated “Last updated” date.
